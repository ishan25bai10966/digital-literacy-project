Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Never share your UPI PIN, OTP, or banking details with anyone
- Do not approve unknown or suspicious payment requests
- Always verify the sender before making any transaction
- Avoid clicking on unknown links or downloading suspicious files
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
- Regularly update your apps and devices for security
- Do not share personal information on untrusted platforms
- Always check payment details carefully before confirming

For reporting cybercrime:
National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
