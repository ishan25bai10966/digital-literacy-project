Social Media Do’s and Don’ts

Do’s:
- Use a professional profile picture
- Share useful and positive content
- Respect others while communicating online
- Keep your profile clean and updated
- Think before posting anything

Don’ts:
- Do not share personal or sensitive information
- Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
- Do not spread fake or unverified information
- Avoid using abusive or disrespectful language
- Do not engage in unnecessary online arguments
